The Legend of Zelda


Here are some facts on one of my favorite games

  1. The Legend of Zelda has been around since the 1980s starting on the NES
  2. It has been on 8 consoles including the newest system the Nintendo Switch
  3. The three main characters are Zelda the princess, Link the soldier/hero, and Ganon / Ganondorf the thief king and great beast
  4. The three main characters all hold a piece of a great power called the Triforce
